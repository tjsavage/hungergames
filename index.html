Ep1: The Oregon Trail

Westward ho!

Sample fare from across the wild west as you embark on a journey to the great unknown. Brave the endless plains, treacherous rivers, and, of course, dysentery, as you search for a new life.

Courses

  • Stocking up at the general store - Buttermilk biscuits, honey butter, various jams
  • Springtime on the prairie - Dandelion greens, puntarelle, bee pollen
  • Summer rations - Corn succotash, baby lima beans, okra, cherry tomatoes, basil
  • A successful hunt - Braised bison short ribs, merlot port reduction, whipped russets
  • An apple a day keeps the cholera away - Apple medley, pecan crumble
  • Winter in the cascades - Caramelized pine nuts, peppermint, vanilla bean ice cream

Highlights

  • A vigorous debate at the general store leads to a poor choice of rations
  • Enthusiastic shotgunning of silver bullets makes for a successful hunt
  • A pitiful traveler gets dysentary, finishes their course in the outhouse
  • Cholera spreads like wildfire on the prairie
